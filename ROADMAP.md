# Termux-Mastery Roadmap

## Introduction

Welcome to the Termux-Mastery Roadmap! This document provides a complete bird's eye view of your learning journey from start to finish. Use this roadmap to track your progress, see what exciting topics are coming up, and understand exactly how each section connects to build your skills in Termux.

## Who This Roadmap Is For

This roadmap is designed strictly for **complete beginners**. You need absolutely no prior knowledge of Linux, coding, or the command line to succeed here. If you know how to use an Android phone and are eager to learn, this path is built for you.

## Recommended Reading Order

To get the most out of this repository, it is highly recommended that you **go in order from 01 to 15**. 

The command line is a compounding skill. What you learn about file navigation in folder 02 will be strictly required to write scripts in folder 07. Skipping around will likely cause confusion, so take your time, trust the process, and follow the numbers step by step.

## Milestones

As you progress through the folders, you will hit major milestones that mark significant leaps in your technical ability. Keep these goals in mind as you learn:

- **MILESTONE 01 — Folders 01 to 03**
  You can now use Termux comfortably, understand the interface, and install new software.
- **MILESTONE 02 — Folders 04 to 06**
  You understand the file system, can edit configurations, and know network basics.
- **MILESTONE 03 — Folders 07 to 09**
  You can write your own shell scripts, code in Python, and back up your work with Git.
- **MILESTONE 04 — Folders 10 to 12**
  You can access remote servers, control Android hardware features, and customize your setup.
- **MILESTONE 05 — Folders 13 to 15**
  You can build real, practical tools, utilize advanced utilities, and troubleshoot your own problems independently.
